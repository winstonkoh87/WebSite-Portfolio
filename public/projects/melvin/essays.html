What Two Years Taught Me

National Service is compulsory for every Singaporean male. But what you take from it is a choice. I went in expecting to just get through it. I came out understanding what it means to lead under pressure. OCS taught me that leadership isn't about being the smartest in the room — it's about being the most trusted. That lesson stays with me.
